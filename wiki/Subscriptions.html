Subscription Plans

AppStudio is available with a monthly or annual subscription. The subscription gives you all updates to new releases, as well as VoltServer.

Free Plan

Lets you upload your apps to VoltServer. Apps will be available for about an hour, which is useful for testing. You'll have to pay the update fee for the next major release of AppStudio.

Essential Plan

Updates to AppStudio are free.

Lets you upload your apps to VoltServer. Apps will be available perpetually while your subscription is in effect, which means you can share them with your customers or friends.

Plus, these services:

  • User Management
  • serverStorage
  • appStorage
  • AdSense
  • Custom Domain names
  • New services as they are added

Pro Plan

You'll get everything in Services, plus Premium Support

Cancelling or Changing your Plan

PayPal

Cancelling or Changing to Free Plan

(If you're on the special $9.95 month plan, you will lose this plan if you switch.)

On PayPal, go to My preapproved payments. Find the AppStudio line, click on it and change the Status to Cancel.

Changing to Essential or Pro Plan

If you have an existing paid plan, cancel it first by going to My preapproved payments. Find the AppStudio item, click on it and change the Status to Cancel.

Go to our Choose your Plan page to sign up for the new plan.
